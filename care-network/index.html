Why we built the Care Network

We can't be everything for our customers. So we built a network of partners we trust.

Hair wellness pulls on five different specialties — dermatology, endocrinology, nutrition, functional medicine, and primary care. No single brand can replicate that expertise. So instead of pretending to, we build the connective tissue: a curated network of partners who fill the gaps Folicelle doesn't.

Our vetting standard is conservative. Every partner is reviewed by Dr. Safahi or our medical advisor before inclusion. We ask: would they recommend this to their own patient? If the answer is "maybe" or "I'd want to look at the methodology more closely," that partner doesn't enter the network until they do.

About commercial transparency: We do receive affiliate revenue from partners on referred orders — typically a percentage of net on at-home testing kits and similar services. We're transparent about this because the alternative — claiming we have "no commercial relationship" with our partners — would be misleading. What matters is that the discounts we negotiate go to you, not into our margin, and the curation decisions are made on quality, not commission.

The Care Network is the medical-adjacent infrastructure women need that doesn't exist anywhere else. It's not a marketplace. It's not a directory. It's a curated set of vetted partners that fits inside the protocol your Toolkit recommends — and you can opt into any of it, none of it, or all of it.

— Dafina
Privacy & independence promise

Your data, your call.

  • Your data is yoursPartner integrations require your explicit, per-partner opt-in. We don't share your assessment answers, photos, or lab results with any partner without your direct permission.
  • Folicelle is independentWe don't take any kickback, commission, or fee on individual customer orders that affects what we recommend. Our recommendations are based on what fits your protocol, not what pays us most.
  • Transparent revenue shareWhere we do earn affiliate revenue (e.g., a small percentage of net on referred at-home testing orders), it's disclosed openly here and in our terms. The discounts go to you. Curation decisions are made on quality.
  • Opt out anytimeYou can opt out of any partnership at any time. Your data with that partner is yours to take elsewhere or delete. We don't lock you into our network.
  • No paid placementsWe don't accept fees from brands or practitioners to enter the network. Inclusion is decided on Dr. Safahi's medical review, not commercial considerations.
