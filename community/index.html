The Four Paths

Find your people, by your stage.

The Cohort is organized by where you are in your hair journey — not by demographics or generic categories. You'll be matched to one path automatically through the Hair Wellness Assessment, and you can opt into a second path if your story spans more than one.

For women navigating the shedding, density shifts, and recovery rhythms of life with a new baby. Conversations here center on what's normal, what isn't, and how to nourish yourself when sleep is rationed.

What happens inside

The conversations that don't happen anywhere else.

The Cohort isn't a forum or a Facebook group. It's a curated space designed around the kinds of conversations women's hair wellness deserves and rarely gets.

01

Peer support, by stage

Members in similar life stages, sharing what's worked. No judgment, no comparison, no medical advice between members — just real reflection on the journey.

02

Safahi office hours

Once a month, our Medical Advisor Dr. Miriam Safahi joins each cohort for a Q&A thread. Members submit questions in advance; she responds in writing.

03

Founder presence

For the first 90 days of every member's journey, Dafina shows up weekly. Reading threads, occasionally responding, available for questions you wouldn't ask a brand.

04

Photo journey, opt-in

Share your day-30, day-60, day-90 photos with your cohort if you want to. Stay private if you don't. The default is private; sharing is always a choice.

Our promise

Curated, not crowded.

The Cohort is moderated, slow, and intentionally small. We'd rather have a thousand engaged members than a hundred thousand passive ones.

  • Trained moderators in every cohort.Real humans, not bots, watching for tone, accuracy, and member wellbeing.
  • Medical content reviewed by Dr. Safahi.Anything medical-adjacent gets routed to her for review before it stays up.
  • No medical advice between members.Members share experiences, never diagnoses. We redirect medical questions to your healthcare provider.
  • Crisis resources, when needed.If a thread surfaces something that needs real support — disordered eating, severe distress — moderators respond with appropriate resources, not platitudes.
  • Privacy-first photo sharing.Photos are private by default. Sharing is opt-in, per-photo. You can revoke access anytime.

"Hair changes are isolating. The Cohort is the antidote."

I started Folicelle because the conversations women need about their hair don't happen in a fifteen-minute appointment. They also don't happen on Reddit or Instagram or in our group chats — at least not the kind I needed.

What I wanted was a small, smart, curated space where women three months ahead of me could tell me what worked. Where Dr. Safahi could weigh in monthly without it being a paid consultation. Where the photos I shared stayed private unless I chose otherwise. That's what The Cohort is.
