The hero ingredient
HE SHOU WU or, "Black-Haired Mr. He"

More than a thousand years ago, Chinese herbal records tell the story of Mr. He — a man whose hair returned to its natural color after years of taking the root of Polygonum multiflorum. The name stuck. The herb has anchored Eastern hair tonics ever since.

Folicelle Foundation is built on that lineage. We pair the heritage botanical with a modern clinical-strength foundation — methylated B-vitamins, the methylated form of folate, copper, zinc, tyrosine, selenium, chlorella — to nourish hair from within at therapeutic dosages.

Dermatologist-reviewed. Naturopath-formulated. Two gummies a day, with a meal. Three to six months for the rhythm to show.

Hair wellness was never the job of one specialty.

I built Folicelle because the conversations women need about their hair don't happen in a fifteen-minute appointment. The hormonal shifts, the nutritional gaps, the stress patterns — they all show up in your hair, but no single doctor's office is set up to read them together.

So I built the framework I wished I'd had. A daily ritual that pairs Eastern heritage with Western clinical science. A platform that teaches you the layers underneath the surface — and gives you the language to walk into any office and be heard.

A Deeper Beauty is the simple idea behind all of it: that real beauty starts with what's beneath, and the wisdom to read it is yours to hold.
