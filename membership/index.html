Who membership serves

For the women already on the journey.

Membership exists for the customer the supplement subscription doesn't fit cleanly. Not as a consolation tier — as a real path that some women genuinely prefer.

  • You're already on a hair wellness journey and want the platform infrastructure around it — community, course, toolkit — without committing to another supplement on top of what you already take.
  • You can't take the supplement right now — pregnancy, breastfeeding, medication interactions, allergies, life context — and you want to stay engaged with the platform until the timing is right.
  • You're not ready to commit to a $61/month effective supplement subscription, but the community and Method course feel worth $19 a month or $179 a year.
  • You completed the standalone $79 Method, your 30-day Cohort trial ended, and you want ongoing community access plus Care Network discounts.

Membership is a real path, not a consolation prize. The community is the same community subscribers are in. The course is the same course. What's missing is the supplement and the Toolkit's deepest features — and we're honest about which features those are.

Plainly answered

What members ask before joining.

You can subscribe to Folicelle Foundation any time and your membership continues alongside it — your platform access doesn't reset, your photo tracker history stays, your cohort identity remains. Subscribers get the full Toolkit (including the Doctor Script Generator and Lab Results Module) automatically. The membership becomes redundant once you subscribe; we'll auto-pause your membership billing so you don't pay twice.

Monthly: yes, one click from your account. Annual: you can cancel at any point but the annual term doesn't pro-rate. We'll process the cancellation immediately, you'll keep access through the end of the paid period, and we won't auto-renew.

The Doctor Script Generator and Lab Results Module are the highest-stakes features of the Toolkit — they touch lab data and shape conversations with your healthcare provider. We tier them with the daily ritual or the full course commitment because they deserve the deeper engagement those paths represent. If the Doctor Script is what you most want, the $79 Method is the most direct path — one-time, lifetime course access, full Toolkit included.

Yes. Trained moderators in every cohort. Medical content reviewed by Dr. Safahi. No medical advice between members — moderators redirect those questions to your healthcare provider. Crisis resources available if needed. Photo sharing is opt-in, per-photo. The full moderation policy lives on the Cohort page.

The Method is a one-time $79 purchase: lifetime course access, 30-day cohort trial, full Toolkit (Doctor Script Generator and Lab Results Module included). It's a knowledge purchase. Membership is ongoing access at $19/month or $179/year: same course, same cohort (full ongoing access, not just trial), basic Toolkit (no Doctor Script or Lab Results), Care Network discounts. It's a community-and-platform subscription. Pick the one that fits the relationship you want with the brand.

The Hair Wellness Protocol PDF is a welcome gift exclusive to the 90-day prepaid Foundation subscription. Members and Method buyers don't receive the PDF, but the same content is integrated into the Method modules — particularly Module 8 (Your Protocol).

If a payment fails, we email you twice over a week and pause your access on day eight. Your data stays in our system for 30 days; you can reactivate without losing your photo tracker history or cohort identity. If you don't reactivate, we delete your data unless you ask us to keep it.

Membership is per-person — it's tied to your assessment results, your cohort, your photo tracker. We'd love for someone you care about to take their own assessment and join their own cohort. If cost is a barrier, write to support; we don't want it to be.
