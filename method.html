Why this exists

A simple truth, the medical system doesn't quite know what to do with.

Hair wellness sits between specialties. Your dermatologist is trained for the scalp. Your endocrinologist for hormones. Your GP for everything and nothing. Your stylist sees the result, but not the cause. So women are left to translate between five appointments and figure out the connections themselves — usually after months of being told it's "probably stress."

The Method is the framework I wish I'd had. The questions to ask. The labs to request. The hormonal, nutritional, and environmental layers that almost always overlap. The language to bring into a doctor's office so the conversation is faster, kinder, and more useful.

It's the inside-out education that pairs with everything else you're already doing for your hair — and stands on its own if you're not yet ready for a daily ritual.

— Dafina

The Environmental Layer

Advocating for Yourself in the Medical System
