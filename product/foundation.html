The Hero · He Shou Wu

He Shou Wu

Polygonum multiflorum · "Black-Haired Mr. He"

More than a thousand years ago, Chinese herbal records tell the story of Mr. He — a man whose hair returned to its natural color after years of taking the root of Polygonum multiflorum. The name stuck. The herb has anchored Eastern hair tonics ever since.

Folicelle Foundation is built on that lineage. He Shou Wu is the heritage foundation. The Western clinical stack — methylated B-vitamins, copper, zinc, tyrosine, selenium, chlorella — provides the modern nutritional support most adult women's hair needs as biology shifts.

Naturopath-formulated. Dermatologist-reviewed. Two gummies a day, with a meal.

The full stack

Biotin

Vitamin B7
Therapeutic dose

Nourishes keratin production, the structural protein in hair. The B-vitamin most associated with hair strand quality.

Methylated B-12

Methylcobalamin
Bioavailable form

The methylated form of B-12 — supports cell turnover and the foundation of healthy follicle activity.

Folate

5-MTHF · methylated

The methylated form of folate. Supports cell division and the conversion of nutrients the follicle relies on.

Vitamin B-6

Supports the metabolic conversions across the B-vitamin family. Works synergistically with B-12 and folate.

Tyrosine

L-Tyrosine
Amino acid precursor

The amino acid precursor to melanin. Supports the body's natural pigmentation processes from within.

Selenium

An antioxidant trace mineral. Supports thyroid function and the systems that affect hair quality from a distance.

Zinc

Copper

Module 04½ · The full picture

Modern life. Ancestral answers.

Modern life puts pressures on women's hair our ancestors didn't face. The herb they used a thousand years ago is still part of the answer — paired with a clinical-strength foundation built for the specific shifts of now.

— 01 —

The Hormonal Chapter

Postpartum · Perimenopause · Post-pill · Thyroid

Hair density and texture often shift first when hormones do. The body needs more support during transitions, not less.

What we built into the formula
  • Methylated B-12 and 5-MTHF folate to support the body's natural processes during transitions
  • Zinc and copper bisglycinate for keratinization and dermal-layer support
  • Tyrosine, the amino-acid precursor to melanin
The heritage anchor He Shou Wu has been used in Eastern medicine to support women through life-stage transitions for over a thousand years.
— 02 —

The Cortisol Curve

Stress · Sleep loss · Telogen effluvium

Stress shows up in hair on a three-month delay. The nutrients the body relies on during high-pressure chapters are the same ones your follicles depend on.

What we built into the formula
  • A complete B-vitamin family (B-6, methylated B-12, methylated folate)
  • Selenium and zinc to support antioxidant and follicle systems
  • Chlorella for whole-food trace mineral support
The heritage anchor He Shou Wu is classified in TCM as a yin tonic — a centuries-old tradition of root tonics taken during high-pressure life chapters.
— 03 —

The Aging Layer

Density · Pigment · Strand quality

As biology shifts, the body's natural pigmentation processes slow, follicle activity changes, and the foundation requires more of certain nutrients than before.

What we built into the formula
  • Tyrosine, the amino-acid precursor to melanin
  • Copper, which supports natural pigmentation and collagen formation
  • Biotin and methylated B-vitamins to nourish the follicle from within
The heritage anchor He Shou Wu — literally "Black-Haired Mr. He" — is the heritage botanical anchored in the ancient Eastern story of hair returning to its natural color.
— 04 —

The Metabolic Transition

GLP-1 · Weight changes · Dietary shifts

Rapid weight changes — through any cause — can shift how the body absorbs nutrients and how follicles cycle. Folicelle Foundation is one piece of a larger conversation with your physician.

What we built into the formula
  • Methylated forms of B-12 and folate (5-MTHF) — bioavailable when absorption shifts
  • Chelated minerals (zinc bisglycinate, copper bisglycinate) — easier on the gut
  • Chlorella — whole-food nutrient density
The heritage anchor He Shou Wu is the foundational botanical Folicelle is built around. If you are taking a GLP-1 medication, please speak with your prescriber before starting any supplement.

Folicelle Foundation is a daily nutritional supplement. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition or disease. The descriptions above explain how the formulation was designed and the traditional use of He Shou Wu in Eastern medicine.

Hair grows in cycles. Visible changes follow biology — not the calendar week. Here's what most women report inside our community.

Rebuilding

The ritual begins. Two gummies a day, with breakfast. Your body is starting to absorb the methylated B-vitamins, copper, and zinc — the foundation underneath.

Early signs

Some women notice subtle texture and quality differences around week six. Many notice nothing yet — both are normal. Hair cycles are slower than skin cycles.
