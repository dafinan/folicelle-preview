Why a week?

Hair takes ninety days. The ritual takes seven.

We made the starter pack because some decisions deserve a slower yes. A week is enough to learn whether the morning ritual fits your life — whether two gummies feel like a small good thing or a small obligation, whether the platform conversations land, whether the brand voice rings true.

A week is not enough to show you what your hair will do. That takes three to six months, because that's how long a hair cycle is. We won't promise visible change in seven days. We'll promise you'll know whether the ritual is right.

The hero ingredient

A 1,000-year-old herb at the heart of every ritual.

He Shou Wu — literally "Black-Haired Mr. He" — is the heritage botanical Folicelle Foundation is built around. More than a thousand years ago, Chinese herbal records told the story of a man whose hair returned to its natural color after years of taking the root. The herb has anchored Eastern hair tonics ever since.

Folicelle pairs this lineage with a clinical-strength foundation — methylated B-vitamins, copper, zinc, tyrosine — built for the way modern women's bodies move now.
