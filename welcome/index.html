Your fourteen days

What unfolds, day by day.

Concrete and specific. Each day adds something — a module, a thread, a decision moment. You'll never feel lost.

  • Module 1 unlocks · How Hair Actually Works

    The biology beneath the surface. Includes the heritage story of He Shou Wu, the 1,000-year-old herb that anchors Folicelle.

  • Module 2 unlocks · Reading Your Body's Signals

    The difference between shedding and thinning, telogen and anagen. How to read what your hair is telling you.

  • Doctor Script generated

    Based on your assessment, the Toolkit drafts a request framework you can take to your GP or dermatologist. Specific labs to discuss, plain-language context.

  • Cohort welcome thread

    You're matched into your cohort by now. A welcome thread invites you to introduce yourself when you're ready — and reading without posting is welcome too.

  • Module 3 unlocks · The Hormonal Layer

    Postpartum, perimenopause, GLP-1, post-pill, thyroid. The hormonal shifts that show up first in your hair.

  • Photo tracker baseline reminder

    A soft prompt to take your three baseline photos — front, top, and crown. Stays private by default; sharing with your cohort is opt-in, per photo.

  • Email arrives

    "Your fourteen days are nearly up." Three options laid out plainly: subscribe to Foundation (platform stays free), upgrade to Membership, or close out the trial. No auto-renewal.

  • Your decision moment

    Whichever path you choose, your trial Toolkit data is preserved if you continue with us. If you don't, we hold it for 30 days then delete unless you ask us to keep it.

Our honest promise

No surprises, ever.

  • No credit card requiredFirst name and email only. The trial is genuinely free — not a "free with $1 charge for shipping" kind of free.
  • Your data is private and encryptedPhotos, assessment answers, and any lab values you enter are stored encrypted at rest. We never share with third parties without your explicit, per-share opt-in.
  • No auto-renewalWe email you on day thirteen asking what you'd like to do. Nothing happens unless you choose it. Closing the trial is a single click — or simply ignoring the email.
  • Trial Toolkit data preserved on subscribeIf you decide to continue with Foundation or Membership, your assessment results, photo tracker, and any lab data carry over. No re-entry, no friction.
  • If you don't subscribe, your data sleeps for 30 days then deletes30 days to change your mind. After that, we delete your data unless you explicitly ask us to keep it. You can also delete it on day one, manually, from your account.

Why Folicelle exists

A platform with a daily ritual at its heart.

Folicelle was built around a simple idea — that hair wellness sits in the gap the medical system doesn't quite fill. Dermatology sees the surface. GPs run general labs. Stylists see the result, not the cause. Women translate between them all, often after months of being told it's "probably stress."

We built the framework we wished we'd had. A daily ritual rooted in He Shou Wu — the 1,000-year-old Eastern hair tonic — paired with a clinical-strength foundation of methylated B-vitamins, copper, zinc, tyrosine, selenium, and chlorella. Around the ritual, a platform: an 8-module course, a community organized by hair stage, a Self-Advocacy Toolkit, and a Care Network of vetted partners.

Your fourteen days will give you the platform. The ritual itself is something you decide about on day fourteen — when you've felt enough of the practice to know whether it belongs in your life.
